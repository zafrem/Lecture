# Vertical RAG

Industry-specific Retrieval-Augmented Generation that utilizes domain-specific datasets to provide highly accurate, relevant, and authoritative information.

## 1. Legal Compliance RAG
**Goal:** Specialized for legal professionals to query vast databases of case law, statutes, and regulatory updates.
- **Workflow**:
    1. Ingesting and indexing proprietary legal documents, court records, and government gazettes.
    2. Context-aware retrieval that prioritizes recent rulings and relevant jurisdictions.
    3. Generating citations and links to original sources for verification.
- **Impact**: Accelerates legal research and ensures compliance in high-stakes environments.

## 2. Medical Diagnostic Support RAG
**Goal:** Assist healthcare professionals with evidence-based insights derived from clinical guidelines and medical journals.
- **Workflow**:
    1. Processing millions of research papers, medical journals, and textbook chapters.
    2. Analyzing patient data (anonymized) to retrieve specific treatment protocols.
    3. Providing summarized summaries of medical consensus on rare conditions or symptoms.
- **Impact**: Improves diagnostic accuracy and supports personalized medicine.

## 3. Financial Market Analysis RAG
**Goal:** Combine real-time market data with historical analyst reports for rapid sentiment analysis and trend prediction.
- **Workflow**:
    1. Real-time ingestion of earnings transcripts, financial news feeds, and market sentiment reports.
    2. Retrieval of historical performance data and comparative industry analysis.
    3. Generating detailed summaries of market trends and potential risks.
- **Impact**: Enables data-driven decision making and provides an edge in volatile markets.
